Detox Drinks (Green Juice):

Detox drinks, especially green juices, have gained popularity for their potential health benefits. These beverages are typically made from a blend of fresh vegetables, fruits, and sometimes herbs or spices. Common ingredients include spinach, kale, cucumber, celery, lemon, ginger, and green apples.


Benefits of Green Detox Juices:

  • 1- Rich in Nutrients
  • 2- Aids Digestion
  • 3- Boosts Hydration
  • 4- Supports Detoxification
